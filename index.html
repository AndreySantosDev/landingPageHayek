Sobre a Hayek Global College

Hayek Global College, accredited by the Ministry of Education in Brazil, is located in a modern corporate complex in the heart of Brasília.
Committed to delivering a truly global education, Hayek combines active learning methodologies with cutting-edge technology to create a rich,
culturally diverse environment where students and professors from around the world come together.

Since 2021, Hayek has offered a Global MBA program designed to equip professionals for success in an interconnected world. Led by a faculty
with extensive international expertise, this program blends practical learning with a global perspective connecting students from over 23
countries in 5 different continents in synchronous virtual classrooms.

For its achievements in the Global MBA program, in June 2024 Hayek was ranked 8th in the world in the World Univeristy Rankings for Innovation
(WURI), in the category Student Mobility and Openness.

In August of 2024, Hayek launched its 2+2 undergraduate program, enabling students to start their studies in Brazil and complete them at a
leading U.S. institution such as Cumberland University, Fort Hays State University, SUNY Alfred State University, or Kentucky State University.
The faculty includes seasoned professionals and academics from five continents, bringing a wealth of global insights that prepare students to
lead and innovate. Through Collaborative Online International Learning (COIL) projects, students also engage with peers from around the world,
developing essential skills to thrive in a globalized workforce.
